Pioneering Gigabit Connectivity

Welcome to SREE DVR Broadband Pvt. Ltd.! We are the region's leading and innovative Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and Internet Service Provider (ISP), licensed by the Department of Telecommunication (DoT).

Our high-capacity fiber optical networks deliver high-speed, low-latency broadband right inside homes, offices, schools, and mandals. By bypassing outdated copper infrastructures, we enable seamless 4K UHD television viewing, corporate leased line setups, and video surveillance streaming.

Choose SREE DVR Broadband for reliable network SLA guarantees, localized customer help desks, and dynamic digital TV packages that need no additional set-top boxes.
